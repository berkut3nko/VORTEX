VORTEX
VORTEX (Voxel Octree Ray-Tracing Engine X) - is a voxel engine built with Vulkan 1.3 and C++20, real-time structural destruction and ray tracing. High-performance GPU data management, enabling advanced volumetric effects and complex physics through the SHRED system.
